Omellete Recipie

Omellete is originally from Spain, and today I am going to show you how you can prepare it with just any normal ingredients that you can find at home

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Coriander

Instructions

  1. Dice the onions and tomatoes into small pieces
  2. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk
  3. Add your tomatoes and onions to the eggs and a pinch of salt
  4. Add oil to your pan and let it heat up before adding the eggs
  5. Add your eggs to the pan and flip the sides to ensure even cooking
  6. Serve onto a plate, spread some coriander and enjoy
